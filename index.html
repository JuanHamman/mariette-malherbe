About Mariette

Meet Your Coach

I'm a certified life and business coach based in Fourways, Johannesburg, with a background in guidance psychology and education. My journey into coaching was shaped by real life — raising two daughters on my own taught me that support, perseverance, and meaningful connection are powerful forces for growth.

Today, my daughters are strong, independent women who navigate life with confidence and clarity. The challenges we faced together shaped the resilience and connection in our relationship — and deeply influenced my coaching practice.

With over 26 years of international training experience, I specialise in helping teens build confidence, parents find calm and connection, individuals gain clarity, and business professionals develop high-impact leadership skills. I work locally in Johannesburg and online throughout South Africa.

A simple, supportive process designed to create meaningful and lasting change.
